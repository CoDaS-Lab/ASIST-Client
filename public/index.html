INSTRUCTIONS.

You will be quizzed on the content of these instructions before you can start the game, so please read the instructions carefully.

There are three rooms at the end of each of the three hallways. One of these rooms has a victim. You are teamed up with a knowledgeable leader who knows where the victim is but cannot talk, and does not have the ability to save the victim. Your goal is to save the victim in the number of rounds and time limit provided.

  • A snapshot of the beginning of the game is shown on the right.
  • Each movement in any direction is counted as one round.
  • You can use the up, down, left, and right key for movement in the game.
  • Victims can be saved from any of the adjacent locations by pressing the “r” key.
  • A room’s view is blocked until you reach its door indicated by the light blue square. The victim is represented by the purple square.

You will be asked to play this game 5 different times. The entire experiment should take about 5 minutes.
