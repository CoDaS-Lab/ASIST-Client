INSTRUCTIONS.

  • The game has three rooms and two victims. Each room can contain at maximum one victim.
  • Player can use the up, down, left, and right key for movement in the game. Each movement of the player in any direction is counted as one round.
  • The goal of the game is to save both victims in 100 rounds and under 2 minutes.
  • Victims are represented by purple colored square boxes that turns yellow when saved. Victims can saved from any of the adjacent location by pressing r key.
  • The room view is blocked to hide the presence of the victim in it. The view can be unblocked as soon as a player reaches the the entrance of the room indicated by light blue square box which makes the victim visible to the player
  • There is a bot in the game who knows which rooms contains the victims and may lead the player to the correct rooms.
